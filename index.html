The Experience

More Than a Meal —
A Journey

Step into a space where Russian warmth and Kuwaiti hospitality merge. Our interiors draw from imperial Russian aesthetics — deep woods, warm amber light, and ornate detail.

Every visit to Masha is an occasion — whether an intimate dinner for two or a celebration with family, we craft moments that linger long after the last course.

  • Authentic imperial-inspired décor and ambiance
  • Curated non-alcoholic traditional Russian beverages
  • Private dining rooms for special occasions
  • Live traditional music on weekends
